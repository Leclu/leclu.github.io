I am Leclu.

I like computers.

As a college freshman in 's-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands, I'm diving into ESD/IT studies while simultaneously working as a courier. My eyes are set on snagging an IT role—maybe as a network manager or diving into device repairs.

And my website? It might seem bare, but that minimalist look? Totally intentional.
